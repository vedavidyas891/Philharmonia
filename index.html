Our Heritage

A Legacy of
Musical Excellence

"Music is the shorthand of emotion — and we dedicate our craft to expressing what words cannot."

Founded in 1947, the Philharmonia Orchestra has been at the forefront of India's classical music landscape for nearly eight decades. From our earliest performances at Kolkata's Grand Hall to our residency at the National Centre for the Performing Arts, we have remained committed to presenting the world's great orchestral repertoire with uncompromising artistic excellence.

Our 120 musicians — drawn from India and across the globe — form an ensemble of rare distinction, under the direction of Music Director Maestro Arjun Krishnamurthy.

♦ Artistic Excellence
We hold ourselves to the highest international standards, performing the full breadth of the orchestral canon with passion, precision, and profound musical insight.
♦ Community & Access
Through subsidised tickets, youth programmes, and free community concerts, we believe world-class orchestral music belongs to everyone.
♦ Musical Education
Our academy nurtures the next generation of Indian orchestral musicians through masterclasses, youth orchestras, and scholarship programmes.
♦ Indian Premieres
We champion new works by Indian composers and regularly programme Indian premieres of major international compositions.

Music Education

Youth Orchestra
Our flagship youth programme for musicians aged 12–22. Full orchestral training, sectionals, and public performances under professional conductors. Auditions held annually each September.

Masterclasses
Intensive masterclasses led by our principal musicians and distinguished international soloists. Open to advanced students and emerging professionals across all orchestral instruments.
♫
School Concerts
Specially designed educational concerts bringing live orchestral music to schools across Karnataka and Maharashtra. Curriculum-linked programmes for students aged 6–18.
