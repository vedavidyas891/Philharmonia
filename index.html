Our Heritage

Our Musical
Journey

"Discover the enchanting world of string instruments — where music comes alive and passion resonates."

Welcome to the Bangalore City Chamber Orchestra (BCCO)! Since our debut in May 2018, we have established ourselves as a premier orchestra known for delivering exceptional music and elegant performances. At BCCO, we are committed to engaging top musical professionals from India and promoting music performance as a viable career choice.

Join us as we present a diverse array of stunning productions featuring both homegrown talent and esteemed international artists. The BCCO is now managed and is nestled in the Philomena Thumboochetty Music Academy — created to honour the legacy of the highly accomplished violinist from Bangalore, the late Smt. Philomena Thumboochetty.

♦ Artistic Excellence
We strive to elevate classical music by connecting with our supporters and delivering exceptional performances that resonate with every audience member.
♦ Education & Legacy
The PTMA was created to honour Smt. Philomena Thumboochetty, sharing BCCO's vision of making Bangalore the epicentre for preserving and enriching orchestral music.
♦ Community
Our goal is to enrich the community with the richness and grandeur of Western classical music, while championing music performance as a viable career for Indian musicians.
♦ International Reach
We bring together homegrown talent and esteemed international artists, fostering a global dialogue around chamber and orchestral music rooted in Bangalore.

Our Mission
Our mission at BCCO is to enrich lives through the power of music. We aim to inspire, educate, and uplift individuals by providing soul-stirring performances that resonate with the heart and mind.

Honouring a Legacy
The Philomena Thumboochetty Music Academy was created to honour the legacy of the highly accomplished violinist from Bangalore, the late Smt. Philomena Thumboochetty — preserving her contribution to Indian classical music.
